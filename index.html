I am an Associate Scientist at NOAA's National Hurricane Center (NHC). I help in the design, development, optimization, and testing of new or revised NHC products.

Previously, I was contucting research at the National Center for Atmospheric Research. I focused on the physical science behind tropical cyclone development, and the social science implications of how the public responds to uncertain intensity forecasts.
